Air India is the national flag carrier of India, currently owned by the Tata Group. It operates a wide range of domestic and international flights using a modern fleet that includes Airbus A320s, Boeing 777s, and 787 Dreamliners. In recent years, the airline has undergone major restructuring and modernization efforts to improve services and expand its global reach. However, in June 2025, Air India faced a tragic incident when Flight AI171 crashed shortly after takeoff from Ahmedabad to London Gatwick, resulting in the loss of over 250 lives. This led to international service suspensions, regulatory scrutiny, and global safety reviews. Despite the setback, the airline has gradually resumed international operations and is working to rebuild trust through stricter safety protocols and improved crew training.
